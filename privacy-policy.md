# Privacy Policy — IG Posts to JSON

**Last updated:** February 22, 2026

## Data Collection

This extension does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## How It Works

- The extension reads publicly visible post information (links, thumbnails, captions) from the currently open page **in your browser**.
- All processing happens **locally** on your device.
- No data is sent to any external server, analytics service, or third party.

## Permissions

- **activeTab**: Used to read the current page when you click the extension icon.
- **host_permissions (instagram.com)**: Required so the content script can access page elements.
